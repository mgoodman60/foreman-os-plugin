# Milestone Tracking Reference

## Purpose
Milestone tracking in the weekly report provides the owner with a clear, at-a-glance view of schedule performance against contractual commitments.

## Milestone Reference Format

### Milestone Status Table
| Milestone | Baseline Date | Projected Date | Actual Date | Variance | Status |
|-----------|--------------|----------------|-------------|----------|--------|
| Notice to Proceed | 01/06/2026 | — | 01/06/2026 | 0 days | Complete ✓ |
| Foundation Complete | 03/15/2026 | 03/15/2026 | — | 0 days | On Track |
| Steel Erection Complete | 05/01/2026 | 05/08/2026 | — | +7 days | At Risk ⚠ |
| Substantial Completion | 09/15/2026 | 09/15/2026 | — | 0 days | On Track |
| Final Completion | 10/15/2026 | 10/15/2026 | — | 0 days | On Track |

### Status Classification
| Status | Criteria | Color |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Complete | Actual date recorded | Green |
| On Track | Projected = Baseline (±2 days) | Green |
| At Risk | Projected > Baseline by 3-14 days | Amber/Yellow |
| Behind | Projected > Baseline by >14 days | Red |
| Ahead | Projected < Baseline | Blue |

## Variance Reporting
For each milestone with non-zero variance:
- **Planned date** vs **Projected date** vs **Actual date** (if complete)
- **Explanation of delta**: What caused the variance?
- **Recovery plan**: If behind, what actions are being taken?
- **Impact assessment**: Does this milestone variance affect downstream milestones or Substantial Completion?

Format: "[Milestone] is currently projected [X] days [ahead of/behind] baseline due to [cause]. [Recovery action / no impact statement]."

## Trend Indicators
Track milestone projection changes over consecutive weekly reports:
- **Stable**: Projected date unchanged for 3+ weeks → confidence is high
- **Improving**: Projected date moving earlier → positive trend
- **Deteriorating**: Projected date moving later → intervention needed
- **Volatile**: Projected date fluctuating → underlying uncertainty, investigate

## Photo Selection for Milestones
- 3-5 progress photos per week showing milestone advancement
- Before/after comparisons when a phase completes
- Aerial/wide shots showing overall project progress monthly
- Close-ups of quality work for owner confidence
- Avoid: cluttered shots, unflattering angles, photos without context

## Upcoming Milestone Preview
In the "Upcoming Work" section of the weekly report:
- List milestones within the next 30 days
- For each: current status, key dependencies, risk factors
- Highlight any milestones requiring owner decisions or actions

## Historical Milestone Tracking
Maintain a running record of milestone date changes:
```
Foundation Complete:
  Original baseline: 03/15/2026
  Week 4 projection: 03/15/2026 (on track)
  Week 6 projection: 03/18/2026 (3-day delay — weather)
  Week 8 projection: 03/15/2026 (recovered via Saturday work)
  Actual: 03/14/2026 (1 day ahead)
```
This history demonstrates proactive schedule management to the owner.
